Walk Dogs at Grant Park

November 3, 2014

2:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Event Description

Volunteers will pick up packages of food from the food bank depository and will deliver it to various locations around the city. Locations include soup kitchens, shelters, etc.

Event Requirements

  • Must be over 18 years old
  • Must have a valid driver's license
  • Must have your own vehicle
  • Must be able to lift at least 15 lbs

Location
